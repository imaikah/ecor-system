What is eCOR?

eCOR is an Online Certificate of Registration Tracking System for Pateros Technological College.

Mission & Vision

Provide students with easy and transparent access to registration status.

Why it was created

To simplify COR request and tracking process for students and administrators.

System Features

  • Request COR online
  • Track COR status
  • Download COR PDF
